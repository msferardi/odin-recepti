French Fries Recipe

Description

If you want to make French Fries, you will need potatoes, oil and a pan

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Oil
  • salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps to make

  • Heat oil in pan
  • put salt in oil
  • Place potatoes in pan
  • Put cheese over cooked french fries
